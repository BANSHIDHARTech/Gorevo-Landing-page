FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much can I save on my electricity bill? +
With a properly sized solar system, you can save up to 90% on your monthly electricity bill. Savings depend on your current consumption, system size, and local tariff rates.
Do I get government subsidy? +
Yes! Under PM Surya Ghar scheme, you can get up to ₹78,000 subsidy on a 3KW system. We handle the complete subsidy documentation and processing for you.
What is the payback period? +
Typically 3–4 years with government subsidy. After that, you enjoy free electricity for 20+ more years, making solar one of the best investments for your home.
Do solar panels work on cloudy weather? +
Yes! Modern solar panels generate electricity even on cloudy days, at reduced efficiency. UP averages 300+ sunny days per year, making solar an ideal investment here.
What is the maintenance cost? +
Solar systems require minimal maintenance, mainly occasional cleaning. Our 25-year performance warranty and after-sales service ensure complete peace of mind.
How long do solar panels last? +
Premium Tier-1 solar panels last 25+ years with less than 0.5% annual degradation. They continue generating electricity well beyond the warranty period.
